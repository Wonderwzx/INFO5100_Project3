How to Choose an Ideal College and Major?

The visualization aims to help viewers to make selections for their ideal future college and major in the US. We examined three datasets of college-related topics, and combined part of the subsets to create the three graphs that together can provide viewers with comprehensive information and practical suggestions. The three data visualizations include a US states map, a spider map, and a raster graph, which display the three most important factors that viewers need to take into consideration: region, school ranking, and major ranking.
