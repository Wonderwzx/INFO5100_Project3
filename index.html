How to Choose an Ideal College and Major?

The first chart is an interactive US map of the number of colleges, tuition, and career pay. This gives you an idea of where to attend college if you would like to get paid off after graduation. Choose from the dropdown bar to see the data of different states.


The left chart shows the major categories and their incomes. Each dot is a mojor in that category. Hover on it to see which major it is. Select from the dropdown to see an animation of each major showing the changes in median income, 25th percentile of earnings and 75th percentile of earnings.
The ranking is also an important factor to consider when choosing college. The right chart shows different scores according to different ranking systems.
